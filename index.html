Welcome to "Mike Tyson's Punch Out"; Momma said Knock You Out!?!

Instructions:
  • At the start of each "new" game you will select a "character" and "three enemies"
  • The first "Player" you select will be "Your Character"; subsequent players will be "The Enemies"
  • You will then select a "Defender" from "The Enemies"
  • Once all characters are selected; the "Attack Options" will become available
  • Fight Mechanics: each "Jab" w ill increase your attack power by the original attack power
  • Once you have banked 20 attack power or more you can perform an "Uppercut"
  • Uppercut's are equal to (Attack Power + (Attack Power / 2) but reduce your Attack Power in Half; use wisely
  • All character's will have Health set to 100; but the Attack and Counter Attacks are randomly calculated when selected
